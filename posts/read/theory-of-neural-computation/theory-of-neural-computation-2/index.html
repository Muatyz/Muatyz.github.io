Storage Capacity#

Capacity Calculation#

$$ \begin{aligned} m_{\nu} = &\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\xi_{i}^{\nu}\tanh{\left(\beta\sum_{\mu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}m_{\mu}\right)}\\ = &\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\xi_{i}^{\nu}\xi_{i}^{1}\tanh{\left[ \beta\left( m_{1} + {\color{red}{\xi_{i}^{\nu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\nu}}} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right) \right]}\\ =& \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\xi_{i}^{\nu}\xi_{i}^{1}\tanh{\left[ \beta\left( m_{1} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right) \right]} {\color{red}{+ \frac{\beta}{N}\sum_{i}\left\{ 1-\tanh^{2}\left[ \beta\left( m_{1} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right) \right] \right\}m_{\nu}}} \end{aligned} $$

еЫ†дЄЇзђђдЇМй°єжЮБе∞П: $$ \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\tanh(\beta x) = \beta[1-\tanh^{2}(\beta x)] $$

дЄ≠ењГжЮБйЩРеЃЪзРЖ

иКВзВєеЭЗеАЉ $\begin{aligned}N^{-1}\sum_{i}\end{aligned}$ иІЖдЄЇ Gaussian еЩ™е£∞ $\begin{aligned}\sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu}\end{aligned}$, жЦєеЈЃдЄЇ $\alpha r$.

$$ \begin{aligned} q &= \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\tanh^{2}\left[\beta\left( m_{1} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right)\right]\\ &= \int\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}z^{2}}\tanh^{2}{[\beta(m_{1} + \sqrt{\alpha r}z)]} \end{aligned} $$

$$ \begin{aligned} m_{\nu} =& \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\xi_{i}^{\nu}\xi_{i}^{1}\tanh{\left[\beta\left( m_{1} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right)\right]} + \beta m_{\nu} - \beta qm_{\nu}\\ \Leftrightarrow m_{\nu} =& \frac{1}{1-\beta(1-q)}\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i}\xi_{i}^{\nu}\xi_{i}^{1}\tanh{\left[\beta\left( m_{1} + \sum_{\mu\neq 1,\nu}\xi_{i}^{\mu}\xi_{i}^{1}m_{\mu} \right)\right]} \end{aligned} $$
